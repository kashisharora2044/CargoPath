Our Modified Dijkstra algorithm assigns each road segment a composite cost that combines physical distance with road roughness and your cargo's fragility score. Fragile goods like tomatoes or electronics are automatically steered away from rough roads, even if that means a slightly longer path.

Each cargo type has a profit-per-km rate. The profit engine multiplies this by total distance and units, then subtracts an estimated transport cost (based on distance and route roughness). The result is shown as Net Profit in INR, along with a breakdown of revenue, cost, and damage risk.
After each route is calculated, Gemini AI generates a three-paragraph explanation: why the chosen route suits your cargo's fragility, which specific segments carry the highest risk, and practical driving recommendations to minimise damage in transit.
