About CargoPath

Built for Smarter
Logistics

CargoPath is a damage-aware cargo routing platform that combines graph algorithms, road-roughness data, and Gemini AI to help transporters make better decisions — protecting fragile goods while maximising profit.

How It Works

Four Steps to a Smarter Route

The Algorithm

Modified Dijkstra's Shortest Path

Standard shortest-path algorithms optimise for distance alone. CargoPath extends Dijkstra's with a composite cost function that fuses distance with road roughness and cargo fragility.

  • Edge weight = distance × (1 + fragility × roughness / 10) — fragile cargo penalises rough roads exponentially.
  • Top-3 alternative routes are computed so you can compare risk vs. distance vs. profit.
  • All 10 major Indian logistics hubs are modelled as graph nodes with real-world distances and roughness ratings.
  • Routes are stored in SQLite and re-usable for historical analysis and pattern discovery.
